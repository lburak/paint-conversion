The number at the end of the description of every matched color is the delta (the mathematical distance between the colors).
A delta of less than 3 (green blocks) represent a very close match, nearly visually indistinguishable.
A delta of less than 7 (yellow blocks) is usually pretty close and can safely be used as a substitute.

The middle square is the color of the control paint(first column), and the surrounding border is the color of the matching paint.
